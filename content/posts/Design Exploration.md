Last step is what we called Ideal Design / Trade-off based design
- Reasons for work in this field 
	- large gap between Design Methods & Detailed parametric representations
	- Role of Embodying a physical device / configuring different functionalitities is often unclear
	- addressing this Duality of Design (ambition of Top Down Design is clear, either supporting wide exploration of conceptual ideas and avoiding biases, or also supporting planning and organisation of design processes, incl. traceability in SE / at the same time, Design needs to be bottom up, i.e. considering detailed constraints)
- Many design processes are co-design of elements and overall structure, in mechanics with numerous interdependecies & also changes to the conceptual solution
	- These design approaches have historically relied on intuition-based standard templates with a finite set of parameters
	- circuit design: co-design process essentially aims to tailor the electromagnetic structures and the active circuit topologies to optimize the collective response of the end-to-end circuit.
	- by choosing a template, we have already narrowed down the design and trade-off space.
	- quite possible that an entire design space that lies outside the scope of these regular template-based geometries can yield significant improvement over traditional solutions
	- for inverse design: The complex impedances of these devices, their ability to amplify, and theirmutual interactions need to be taken into account to realize an optimized endto-end design process.
- Other direction Computational Exploration (asking the question of well-structured)
	- Complete exploration of design spaces is often computationally prohibitive
	- The role of the designer therefore shifts from the generation of solutions to the definition of design problems and the evaluation of solutions with design options generated and scored computationally (Biskjaer, Dalsgaard & Halskov 2014).
	- engineers use design exploration (often referred to as meta-heuristics) and optimisation algorithms that intelligently navigate the problem space to find optimal solutions.
